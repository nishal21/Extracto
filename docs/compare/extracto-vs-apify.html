Extracto vs. Apify: The 2026 Comparison

The TL;DR: Apify is a massive enterprise cloud platform charging by the compute minute. Extracto is a completely free, open-source Python tool that runs entirely locally and uses AI (GPT-4o, Mistral, Ollama) to parse websites without CSS selectors.

Why Choose Extracto?

If you don't want to lock yourself into a massive cloud ecosystem and pay monthly subscriptions just to extract tables from a website, Extracto is the ultimate Python alternative to Apify. It integrates Crawlee and Playwright under the hood locally, letting you define schema in plain english (`"Get all company names"`).
