Why Choose Extracto?

While ScrapeGraphAI brought LLM scraping to the mainstream, it struggles in real-world scenarios where websites aggressively block bots or rely heavily on React/Vue dynamic rendering. Extracto solves this by utilizing crawlee's anti-bot evasions and full headless browser simulation BEFORE handing the visual data to GPT-4o, Mistral, or Ollama.
